Capstone Project

ABC Restaurant Human Capital Management System

Project Overview

ABC Restaurant HCM is a capstone project aimed at creating an affordable and user-friendly Human Capital Management system for small to medium-sized restaurants. The project focuses on centralizing scheduling, payroll, employee availability, requests, and workforce management into a single platform.

Project Vision

The vision of the project is to provide a simpler and more accessible alternative to enterprise HCM tools for smaller restaurants that often rely on manual scheduling, spreadsheets, and disconnected payroll processes.

Key Features

  • Employee scheduling and payroll management
  • Availability submission and schedule viewing
  • Shift swaps, time-off requests, and reimbursements
  • Manager approvals and workflow management
  • Notifications and reporting support

My Contribution

My work on the capstone involved contributing to software development tasks, feature work, and documentation across the project lifecycle. Based on the project plan and status reports, I was involved in scheduling, payroll, employee workflows, notification-related work, and technical/project deliverables.
