About Me

My background, goals, and professional direction.

Bio

I am currently pursuing an Advanced Diploma in Computer Programming and Analysis at George Brown College. Over the course of my studies, I have developed skills in full stack development, mobile application development, backend systems, and software design. My academic work has exposed me to technologies such as React, MongoDB, JavaScript, ASP.NET, Docker, Java, C#, Python, Android, and iOS development.

In addition to my technical learning, my work experience in customer-facing and fast-paced environments has strengthened my communication, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities. I value continuous learning and enjoy building practical applications that solve real problems.

My goal is to build a career as a full stack developer and contribute to projects that combine strong backend systems with user-friendly frontend experiences. I am motivated by the process of solving problems, learning new technologies, and continuously improving the way software serves people and businesses.

Through my coursework, projects, and capstone experience, I have grown from learning programming fundamentals to building applications across web, backend, and mobile environments. I aim to continue developing my skills, work collaboratively with strong teams, and contribute to scalable, reliable, and meaningful software solutions.
